Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use Zygotrip:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you register.
  • Booking Information: Check-in/check-out dates, guest details, room preferences, and payment data.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, search queries, IP address, browser type, and device information.
  • Communications: Messages you send to us or to property owners through our platform.
  • Location Data: With your permission, precise location for nearby property discovery.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your hotel bookings.
  • To send booking confirmations, invoices, and important updates.
  • To personalize your search results and recommendations.
  • To process payments and manage your wallet balance.
  • To communicate promotional offers and discounts (you may opt out at any time).
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • To improve our platform and develop new features.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Property Owners: Booking details (name, contact, dates) are shared with the hotel/property you book with.
  • Payment Processors: We use secure third-party payment gateways (Razorpay, Stripe) that receive transaction data.
  • Service Providers: Trusted vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, and customer support under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect legal rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Encrypted storage of passwords (bcrypt hashing).
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in between sessions.
  • Remember your search preferences and filters.
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns (via Google Analytics).
  • Show you relevant offers and promotions.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impact platform functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zygotrip.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Booking records are retained for 7 years for legal and financial compliance. You may request deletion of non-essential data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Zygotrip is not directed to children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or a prominent notice on our platform at least 30 days before the change takes effect. Your continued use of Zygotrip after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
